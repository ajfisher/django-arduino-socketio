Django - Arduino - Web Sockets interaction demos

Here are a series of demos that show you how to use web sockets with django and interact with arduino devices of various means

Basics

View your own phone data - this will test that your phone can use the device API.

View the data from the phone - fire this up on your desktop browser to see the data coming from your phone (switch this on before you fire up the next demo on your phone)

Use a phone to send data - make sure your desktop browser is on the link above before you go here on your mobile device

Interaction demos

These demos show how you can interact by using a phone with an arduino. The relevant circuits etc are located in the readme file - more details of which are at https://github.com/ajfisher/django-arduino-socketio

Light Play - change the colour of a light based on the orientation of your phone.
